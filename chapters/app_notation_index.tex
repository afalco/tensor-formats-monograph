\section*{Norm conditions and crossnorms}
%------------------------------------------------------------

\noindent
The following conditions on a norm $\|\cdot\|_D$ on $\VD$ are used
throughout; they are formally introduced in
Chapter~\ref{chap:minsubspaces} and App.~\ref{app:functional}.

\medskip
\begin{tabular}{@{}p{4.5cm}p{7cm}l@{}}
\condCROSS & $\|\bigotimes_j v_j\|_D = \prod_j \|v_j\|_j$
    (crossnorm condition) & Ch.~\ref{chap:algtensor} \\
\condINJ & $\|\cdot\|_D \geq \|\cdot\|_\varepsilon$
    (reasonable crossnorm / injective condition)
    & Ch.~\ref{chap:minsubspaces} \\
$\|\bv\|_\varepsilon$ & injective (spatial / Grothendieck) norm
    & Ch.~\ref{chap:minsubspaces} \\
$\|\bv\|_\pi$ & projective (nuclear) norm & Ch.~\ref{chap:algtensor} \\
$\|\cdot\|_H$ & Hilbert tensor norm on $\bigotimes_j H_j$
    & App.~\ref{app:functional} \\
$\condINJ[T_D]$ & $T_D$-local version of \condINJ, one condition
    per level of tree $\TD$ & Ch.~\ref{chap:topTBT} \\
\end{tabular}

\section*{Assumptions and conditions (summary)}
%------------------------------------------------------------

\noindent
The following labelled conditions appear repeatedly across chapters.
A condition tagged with $[\TD]$ indicates the tree-based version, which
specialises to the Tucker version when $\TD = \TDtuck$.

\medskip
\begin{tabular}{@{}p{4.5cm}p{7cm}l@{}}
\condCROSS & tensor product map is continuous ($\|\cdot\|_D$ is
    a crossnorm) & Ch.~\ref{chap:algtensor} \\
\condINJ & $\|\cdot\|_D \geq \|\cdot\|_\varepsilon$ (reasonable
    crossnorm; implies \condCROSS) & Ch.~\ref{chap:minsubspaces} \\
$\condINJ[T_D]$ & \condINJ\ holds at every level of $\TD$:
    $\|\cdot\|_{\alpha} \geq \|\cdot\|_{\varepsilon,\alpha}$ for
    $\alpha \in \TD$ & Ch.~\ref{chap:topTBT} \\
\end{tabular}
